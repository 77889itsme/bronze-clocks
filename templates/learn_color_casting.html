Phase 1: Casting


Bronzeware is made from copper, tin, and lead, each contributing to its color.

Copper (Cu) gives bronze its golden-brown hue.
Tin (Sn) adds a silvery metallic tone, brightening the overall color.
Lead (Pb) can introduce a duller, grayish tint, while also aiding in the casting process.
