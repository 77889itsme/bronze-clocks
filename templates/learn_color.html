Color



Each piece originally had a distinct color when it was first forged. Over time, archaeological conditions and chemical reactions transformed these surfaces.

There are 3 phases regarding the color of a bronzeware piece. The evolving colors of ancient Chinese bronzeware are deeply connected to changes in alloy composition, advances in casting techniques, and long-term patination processes.
